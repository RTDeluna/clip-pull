CLIP.PULL

Skool Video Downloader

A Chrome extension that detects the video on a Skool lesson page and sends it straight into Clip.Pull's Queue tab — no copy-pasting links.

Install in Chrome
  1. Unzip the downloaded file
  2. Open chrome://extensions
  3. Enable Developer Mode (top right)
  4. Click Load unpacked
  5. Select the unzipped folder

Once it's installed, open any course lesson with a video and click the CLIP.PULL button the extension adds to your toolbar — the video drops straight into your Queue, ready to download.
